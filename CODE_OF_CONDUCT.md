# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
We want Recon Workspace to be a calm, respectful place to collaborate. Contributors are expected to act in good faith, communicate clearly, and avoid behavior that makes participation harder for others.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful in disagreement.
- Assume curiosity before hostility.
- Give actionable, technical feedback instead of personal criticism.
- Respect boundaries around security reports, private data, and incomplete work.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or sustained hostility
- Publishing someone else's private information without permission
- Bad-faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct, and may limit repository participation when needed to keep collaboration safe and productive.

## Scope
This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project communication channels.
