# Blog SEO Writing Guide

## Brand and positioning
- Brand name: `Recon Workspace`
- Product description: `Reconciliation workspace for bookkeepers and small finance teams`
- Primary audience: bookkeepers, bookkeeping agencies, small finance teams, fractional controllers
- Primary product CTA: `Open the sample workspace` linking to `/app`

## Article goals
- Bring in readers who are actively searching for reconciliation workflow help
- Answer the workflow question clearly before pitching the product
- Create a natural bridge from article pain to product trial inside our own site
- Keep CTA traffic on our pages only

## Metadata rules
- Title length: 50-65 characters
- Meta description length: 140-160 characters
- Excerpt length: 90-220 characters
- One canonical URL per article using `SITE_ORIGIN + /blog/<slug>/`
- Page title format: `<Article Title> | Recon Workspace`

## Structure rules
- English first
- No H1 inside the Markdown body; the page template renders the only H1
- The first 100 words must answer the search intent directly
- Target 1,200-1,800 words for most posts
- Target 1,500-2,200 words for checklists, comparisons, or deeper workflows
- Use H2 sections around real reader questions, not vague filler headings
- Use tables or lists when they make the workflow easier to scan

## Content rules
- Stay practical and operational
- Talk about files, review states, missing support, handoff quality, and follow-up
- Explain the real bottleneck before introducing the product
- Mention Recon Workspace naturally as the workflow environment, not as a forced pitch
- Include at least 2 internal links
- Keep product CTA language internal-only

## CTA rules
- Primary CTA: `Open the sample workspace`
- Allowed support link: `/`
- Allowed product CTA link: `/app`
- Do not hardcode a production domain inside content
- Do not send readers to external booking pages, Calendly, or unrelated assets from blog CTAs

## Image rules
- Every post needs exactly 1 hero image
- Store image files in `public/blog/images/<slug>.jpg`
- Use the same image for the article hero and social preview
- Images should be editorial, calm, and text-free
- Preferred framing: wide landscape composition that leaves room for cropping

## Topic filters
- Strong topics:
  - month-end close workflow
  - missing receipt process
  - missing supporting documents
  - reconciliation exception tracking
  - bank statement cleanup workflow
- Weak topics:
  - broad accounting theory with no workflow hook
  - tax-heavy topics outside our product wedge
  - enterprise close management categories that do not map to current product scope

## Quality checklist before publish
- Title and meta description are inside range
- Article meets word-count target
- Markdown body has no H1
- Article includes 2 or more internal links
- Image exists and alt text is specific
- CTA points to `/app`
- No hardcoded domain references
