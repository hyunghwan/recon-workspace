# Content Marketing Plan

## Positioning
Recon Workspace helps bookkeepers and small finance teams manage the messy reconciliation layer before the books are clean.

## Core message pillars
1. Reconciliation is not just matching — it is exception handling.
2. Missing documents are a workflow problem, not just an accounting problem.
3. Small teams are stuck between spreadsheets and enterprise close software.
4. Month-end speed improves when unresolved items are visible early.

## SEO content ideas
- How to reconcile bank statements faster without missing supporting docs
- Missing receipt workflow for bookkeepers
- Month-end close checklist for small bookkeeping teams
- How to track unresolved reconciliation exceptions
- Why reconciliation breaks for messy SMB clients
- Dext vs Hubdoc vs spreadsheets for missing document follow-up
- Statement reconciliation workflow for bookkeeping agencies

## Build-in-public ideas
- before/after UX teardown of spreadsheet-based reconciliation
- short clips showing unresolved-item workflow
- annotated screenshots: what makes month-end chaotic
- “3 things that slow down close for bookkeepers” threads

## Lead magnet ideas
- free month-end close checklist
- free missing receipt email templates
- free reconciliation exception tracker template

- Try the demo workspace
- Download the checklist

Mid-funnel CTA:
