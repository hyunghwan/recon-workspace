# GTM Outline

## First target user
Small bookkeeping agencies and outsourced bookkeepers handling messy monthly close prep for SMB clients.

## Why this target
- recurring pain
- easy ROI story
- one user can bring multiple clients/workspaces
- already familiar with paying for workflow tools

## Initial messaging
### Headline ideas
- Reconcile faster. Chase fewer missing docs.
- The workspace for messy month-end reconciliation.
- Stop managing reconciliation exceptions in spreadsheets.

## Entry hooks
### SEO / content
- statement reconciliation workflow
- missing receipt follow-up process
- bookkeeping month-end checklist
- how to reconcile bank statements faster
- bookkeeping exception tracking

### Outreach-lite
- share working prototype with bookkeeping communities
- short teardown videos of messy reconciliation workflows
- post sample before/after workflows on X / LinkedIn

## Validation assets
- landing page with clear wedge
- interactive demo with sample workspace
- one-page summary PDF later

## Early conversion goal
Do not optimize for self-serve revenue first.
Optimize for:
- bookkeeper interviews
- demo requests
- waitlist signups
- feedback on workflow and pricing

## Pricing hypothesis
Potential future models:
- per workspace/client
- per seat + workspace limit
- per volume band of transactions

## Validation milestone
A strong early signal would be 5-10 bookkeepers saying:
- yes, I do this in spreadsheets today
- yes, this is painful enough
- yes, I would try this with a client next month
