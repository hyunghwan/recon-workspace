# How small bookkeeping teams can speed up month-end close

Month-end close usually does not break because the accounting software failed. It breaks because the supporting workflow around reconciliation is messy.

## The real bottleneck
Small bookkeeping teams often deal with:
- bank and card statements in different formats
- receipts scattered across inboxes, chats, and cloud folders
- unclear transactions that sit in notes instead of a shared workflow
- missing support that gets chased too late

## What actually slows the close
### 1. Unresolved items are not visible early
By the time the close starts, the team is already reacting.

### 2. Missing docs are managed in email, not in a system
The follow-up history is fragmented, so people repeat requests or miss them entirely.

### 3. Exceptions live in spreadsheets with weak context
A line item may have a note, but not an audit trail.

## Better workflow
A better reconciliation prep workflow should:
- track each transaction by status
- separate matched items from unresolved items
- make missing support obvious
- keep notes and activity in one place
- produce a clean unresolved-items summary before month-end

## What to look for in a tool
- easy statement import
- status-based reconciliation workflow
- exception visibility
- support-doc tracking
- exportable unresolved summary

Recon Workspace is being built around exactly this pain: the messy layer before the books are actually clean.
